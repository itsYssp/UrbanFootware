About Us

Welcome to Urban Footwear, your number one source for high-quality shoes. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best footwear, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2022 by Mang Kanor, Urban Footwear has come a long way from its beginnings in Dasmariñas, Cavite. When Mang Kanor first started out, their passion for providing the best shoes for every occasion drove them to do intense research and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Philippines, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
